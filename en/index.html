Welcome to the CLTL pages

We work on computational models of natural language in a large variety of topics including lexicons, ontologies, word embeddings, NLP for digital humanities and social science, event detection and coreference, opinion and argumentation mining, grounding and human-robot interaction and more. You can find out more about our group on our website.

About this website

We create a lot of software and have a policy of sharing what we create. We are proud of presenting our repositories here. See you on GitHub for our latest additions.
