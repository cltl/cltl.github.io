Sentiment and Opinion

OpeNER Corpus

In the OpeNER project, we have created a corpus of news articles and hotel reviews annotated with opinion expressions, holders, targets and their relations.

Opinion Mining

The Opinion Miner Deluxe ++ is an improved version of the first version of the opinion miner based on machine learning that can be trained using a list of KAF/NAF files.
