Python for text analysis

Python for text analysis is a practical course, geared towards those who want to get some hands-on experience working with language data.

This course is now taught at the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam in the Humanities Research Master: Linguistics (track Human Language Technology), and in the Minor Digital Humanities and Social Analytics (BA).
